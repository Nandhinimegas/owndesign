"Patchwork Power, Hour by Hour!"

Repairing clothing can also add character to your clothing pieces. Stitching or repairing your clothing can add a new personal touch.These include holes, tears, missing buttons, or permanent stains. A good way to decrease the amount of clothing that is thrown away whilst also a great cost-saving tip is to repair clothing. Buying new pieces isn’t always the better option.

Repair your clothes is an empowering and creative way to maintain a sustainable wardrobe.Our style of repairing clothing, are visible mending, and invisible mending.

Repairing works

  • Hand Sewing Clothing Repair.
  • Patching Clothes Repair.
  • Fixing Zippers.
