Our Journey

“Beauty is about finding the perfect fabric.”

We Established our local store (offline) in the year 1995 in Erode and we stepped into the digital world with this website, discontinuing the offline shop. Our aims at providing hassle free and enjoyable tailoring experience with all new designs and patterns with a team of talented designers. We provides amazing tailoring experience with one stop solution for all kind of stitching works. We provide stitching services with customisable designs for salwar suits, blouses, kurtas, bottoms, western wear and kids wear as per customers measurement.

We have an unstoppable dedication and customers can rely on us to get a high-quality job done. We respond to customer queries promptly. Throughout three years, we have served thousands of customers, and we thank you for your support in helping our business grow.
