"Transforming Fabrics, Inspiring Lives"

SelviTailoring Center has skilled seamstresses to help you with all major and minor alterations needs. Whether a simple hem, or a custom made repair, our team can tailor your request.

  1. The act of altering or changing something. The changing of a pattern, or design to improve the outcome of the appearance or fit.
  2. The modifications made to a ready made garment or something that has been altered to provide a better fit.

Alteration work under

  • Hemming with a blind stitch.
  • Taking in a seam.
  • Letting out a seam.
  • Adding darts.
  • Lengthen tor shorten the body of a top to better fit your body type.
  • Modifying an actual pattern to include your own preferred design elements or modify to better fit your body type.
